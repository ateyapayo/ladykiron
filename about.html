Welcome!
My name is Amelia, and my story helps to build the foundation for the idea behind this blog.
At the age of 12 I was diagnosed with an autoimmune disease and have had to deal with other associated health problems throughout my life. My disease isn’t curable, but it’s taught me many valuable lessons about health, wellness and spirituality along my journey.
In Greek mythology, Chiron was known as the wounded healer, and in modern astrology it comes to take the same meaning. Chiron was a healer who was excellent at using his own unhealable wounds to heal others, transforming and alchemising his own pain through this process. His story tells us that suffering can be used for good if one so chooses to take a similar path.
My hope is that the information in this blog contains its own healing properties for its readers.
